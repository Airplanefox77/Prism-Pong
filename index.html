Quick Start

Return the ball to build streak and prism charge. In AI mode you score by beating the right paddle; misses increase when the AI scores on you.

Combos

Input 4-key combos within the combo window to prime special shots:

A D D APrism Zigzag
A D D DDown Burst
D A A AUp Burst

Prism Meter

Returns fill your meter. Higher meter adds shot power and score multiplier. At 100% you enter Overexpose Mode for stronger bonuses until it fades.

Random Events

Large Ball, Rainbow Ball, Freeze Ball, and Sticky Ball can change tempo and scoring. Duel mode disables random events.

Pause & Modes

Press Esc or P to open pause. Use Game Mode in Graphics to swap between Classic, Chaos, Duel, and Light Survival.
